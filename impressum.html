Professional titles and regulation

Dr. Philip Weisshuhn is admitted as Patentanwalt (patent attorney) in the Federal Republic of Germany and is a member of the Patentanwaltskammer (German Chamber of Patent Attorneys), Munich. The applicable professional rules, in particular the Patentanwaltsordnung (PAO) and the Berufsordnung der Patentanwälte, are available from the Patentanwaltskammer.

He is admitted as a professional representative before the European Patent Office (reg. no. 9318880). As a member of the Institute of Professional Representatives before the European Patent Office (epi), he is subject to the epi Code of Professional Conduct.

He is admitted as a professional representative before the European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO).

He is a representative before the Unified Patent Court.
