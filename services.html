Services

Typical engagements include:

Oppositions, appeals and litigation

  • Opposition and appeal proceedings before the European Patent Office (EPO)
  • Representation in infringement and revocation proceedings before the Unified Patent Court (UPC)
  • Argument-graph-based case analysis: every line of attack and defence mapped, weighted and stress-tested
  • Second opinions on pending or anticipated proceedings

Prosecution and portfolio

  • Strategic drafting and prosecution of patent applications before the EPO and the German Patent and Trade Mark Office (DPMA)
  • Portfolio review and alignment of the patent estate with business needs
  • Exclusivity and Supplementary Protection Certificates for pharmaceutical compounds
  • Filing and prosecution of trademarks and designs before the DPMA and the European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO)

Transactions and licensing

  • IP due diligence for financing rounds, licensing and M&A
  • Negotiation of IP terms in collaboration and licence agreements
  • IP support for spin-outs and technology transfer

Legal technology

  • Computational analysis of opposition and appeal cases based on formal argument graphs
  • AI-assisted prior art research and case preparation
  • Advice for patent departments on AI-supported IP workflows
